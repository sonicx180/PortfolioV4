About Me

Who I am

I am a fifteen-year-old Christian homeschooler that graduated high school at the age of 14. My name is Conner Ow (pernounced "ow" as if you got hurt) and I am usually known accross the internet as "LeviathanProgramming".

I live way out in the country in Texas and really enjoy that way of life as well of just chilling out in front of the computer screen and coding. That's why I'm a redneck fullstack web developer.

How I started coding

My mom's midwife, who had come to help her deliver my little brother, introduced me to Khan Academy. I got really interested in programming and started to make small games, drawing, and other things of no apparent use on there. That was when I decided to become a programmer. After about a year of playing around with that, I moved on to FreeCodeCamp. That site was the main source of learning I had. I learned so much from there.

Skip ahead a couple of years and you'll find me learning a lot of coding concepts and trying to expand my knowledge. I still am constantly trying to learn and build.

All thanks to my little brother.
